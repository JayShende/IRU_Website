Common Guidelines :

1. The contest is open for the following categories
5th to 8th Standard
9th to 12th Standard
Undergraduate Students
Post Graduate Students and Working Professionals

2. A team has to register in advance to participate in IRU 2023 of YCCE through online mode. The teams are required to register online, latest by 31st December, 2022.

3. Free food shall be provided to all the team members (max. 4 students and 1 faculty), for each category participating in IRU.

4. Free accommodation shall be provided to all the team members (max. 4 students and 1 faculty) for each category participating in IRU, who qualify for the Infinite Pitch, or get selected to win a prize, according to availability.

5. All the participants and mentors selected for the final level shall be awarded participation certificates.
