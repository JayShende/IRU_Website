No Refund Policy:

No refund shall be provided. You can make the request for registration cancellation to Innovations R Us support team by mail at organiser@innovationsrus.in. The Refund on cancellation of tickets are subject to be determined by the organizing team and may vary during the course. Currently, no refund will be provided under any circumstance.

If, at any time during the event it is cancelled, postponed, suspended or delayed for any reason, Innovations R Us team will communicate on updated policies on refund or anything else applicable. The right for the same rests with the organizing team.

It is your responsibility to ascertain whether an event has been cancelled and the date and time of any rearranged event. If an event is cancelled or rescheduled, we will use reasonable endeavours to notify the registered teams of the cancellation once we have received the relevant authorisation from the Team.

Registrations are accepted subject to the Rules and Regulations of the venue and circumstances. Full details are already available from the team. Breach of any of these Rules and Regulations or any unacceptable behaviour likely to cause damage, nuisance or injury shall entitle the venue or Innovations R Us to eject you from the venue. Organizing Team may on occasions have to conduct security searches to ensure the safety of the patrons.
